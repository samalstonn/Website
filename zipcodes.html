Mapping Zipcodes

Final Product

In this project, I used Python, Atom, Git, Matplotlib, numpy, and pandas to take zipcodes from an excel spreadsheet and project them onto a map of the United States. My inspiration for this project was seeing my dad pay $500 a year for a similar software. I decided I could do a better job and devised a solution that was faster, cleaner, and free! This project taught me a lot about using online libraries in order to help me out in my own work. It gave me great experience reading online documentation and formulating a plan for a project where I had no instructions.
